About the Tours

True locals.
Real trails.

Country Roads ATV Tours is run by people who grew up in these mountains — the same family behind the retreat. We're not reading from a script. We know these trails because we've been riding them our whole lives.

We cover the Delbarton, Gilbert, Matewan & Williamson areas with routes through Logan, McDowell, and Eastern Kentucky — including outlaw trails that don't appear on any map. Groups large and small welcome. Book through Facebook Messenger.
